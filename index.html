BRAINSCORES



ABOUT

Brainscores aims to provide an AI-powered collaborative platform to foster collaboration among experts at the patient level, cut down radiological examination times, and enhance clinical diagnosis. In alignment with HBHLâ€™s objectives, the platform leverages multimodal imaging along with AI-assisted and consensus-based diagnostics to improve clinical decision-making. Brainscores has an immense potential to increase patient wellbeing and reduce healthcare costs.

STATUS

Currently, Brainscores is undergoing prototype development. We are also discussing features with stakeholders and partners for a pilot project as the first use case for preliminary validation of the platform. In the next 12 months, we would like to have a prototype with most of the planned features, validation with early adopters, and a clear strategy to work towards a spin-off.
