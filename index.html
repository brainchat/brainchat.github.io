BRAINSCORES | Collaborative Radiology



ABOUT

Brainscores is a novel collaborative and AI powered radiology and medical image analysis platform, that aims to revolutionize diganostics for the most common brain disorders. Developed by experts in medical imaging, AI, and software engineering, Brainscores will provide completely unprecedented ways for doctors and medical specialists to collaborate and exchange patient medical imaging information, and to take advantage of the latest generation of AI technology. This will ultimately enhance clinical diagnosis and cut down examination times.

PROJECT STATUS

Our prototype is currently being developed, hand in hand with clinical and AI experts. We are confident to launch the usuability trials in summer 2023. In the next 12 months, we will then validate the platform with our partners

TECHNOLOGY

Our software platform provides an easy to use interface that leverages the following modules:

  • MultiStream: Multimodal data processing (T1-weighted, FLAIR, Diffusion, Microstructure, Functional)
  • MultiFuse: Multimodal data integration into a common view
  • ImageCollab: Chat functionality at the level of imaging data
  • BrainScorer: Normative model image diagnostic
  • AIAssist: AI technology to highlight significant deviations
  • CaseReporter: Automated radiological report generator
